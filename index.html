General Information

Balmedie is a large village located in the northeast of Scotland in Aberdeenshire, it is about 10 miles north of the city of Aberdeen. Balmedie has scenic views as it has a beach with a sanddune system that stretches 14 miles located next to the Northsea, so its great to watch sunrises at the beach. Balmedie is home approximately 2400 people and is perfect for families as there are childminders, nurseries, a primary school and a coach service or public buses to the nearest secondary schools with afterschool activities at the school or at the Balmedie Leisure Center. Balmedie is on a great bus route which goes from the City Center to Ellon or The City Center to Peterhead. The buses from Balmedie to Aberdeencity are hourly from 6am-9am and then semi hourly from 9am-3pm and then hourly until 8pm and then buses ever 90 minutes from 8pm-11pm, it is similar for going into balmedie, for updated bus times check local busstop or the stagecoach website, Balmedie as a range of restaurant, a library, bakeries, car mechanic, old folks home, pharmacy, dog walkers, hairdresser, nail technician, first aid training, florists, photographers, fitness classes, churches
