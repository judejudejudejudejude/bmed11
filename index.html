Balmedie is a large village located in the northeast of Scotland in Aberdeenshire, it is about 10 miles north of the city of Aberdeen. Balmedie has scenic views as it has a beach with a sanddune system that stretches 14miles located next to the Northsea, so its great to watch sunrises at the beach. Balmedie is home approximately 2400 people and is perfect for families as there are nurseries, a primary school and a coach service or public buses to the nearest secondary schools. Balmedie is on a great bus route which goes from the City Center to Ellon or The City Center to Peterhead.
