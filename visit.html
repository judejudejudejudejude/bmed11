Things to do in Balmedie

The food in Balmedie ranges between Tasty Take-aways to luxury fine dining. Great Bakes can be bought bakeries and sandwiches can be bought shops which you can take to the country park to have great picnics on the benches. In the summer time the shops offer disposable BBQ's, that can brought to the beach where you can cook burgers and hotdogs, just remember to take a binbag and clean up after yourself. All of the restaurant's can work around allergen requests and it's best to book in advance.

Attractions

  • Balmedie is full of many parks for children
  • Go on a walk and explore the woods, country park, sand dunes and beach
  • Visit The Trump International Golf Links and learn or play some golf
  • Check the Balmedie Leisure center for acitivities to such as Bingo, Art, fitness and more!

Food and dining

  • Cafe's: The Sand Bothy and Beachside Coffee Shop
  • Take-away's: Balmedie Fish & Chip Shop and Delice chinese takeaway
  • Bakerie's: Beachside Bakes and Vegan Bay Baker
  • Restaurant's: The Cock & Bull, The White Horse Inn, The Dunes Restaurant & Bar and Trumps Restaurant & Cellar
  • Grocery shops: Co-op and Premier
